Who We Are

Welcome to
Basant Rubber Factory Private Ltd

Established in 1960, Basant Rubber Factory Private Limited has come a long way from supplying rubber goods for horse-driven carriages to addressing the requirement of modern times by evolving into a multi-product company.

An ISO-9001-2015 Quality System Certified company, Basant Rubber Factory develops, manufactures and markets wide range of rubber extruded, moulded and rubber to metal bonded products. These products, known for their quality and performance, are extensively used in Indian Households, Railways, Automobile, Defence and Civil Engineering industries.

Led by a futuristic vision, our diversity in machinery and the experience of our team, the company has developed a strong integration of in-house analytical methods manufacturing processes to satisfy the needs of our customers.

Headquartered in Mumbai, India, Basant Rubber Factory has engineered its growth in several industries with an objective to provide exceptional deliverables and quality. Hence, we see ourselves not just as your supplier but as your reliable innovative partner.
