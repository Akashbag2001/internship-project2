Our Facilities

Basant Rubber Factory’s state-of-the-art machinery reflects our commitment to quality and reliability. The machinery and equipment used in our facility come from the world’s most reputed and trusted machine manufacturers such as Klockner-Desma, Larsen & Toubro, Dak Systems and so on.

Our Testing Equipment:

Our in-house testing laboratory carries out extensive tests and checks from the raw material inspection stage right up to final product assembly. Our Quality Control department is equipped with the latest test equipment such as:

  • Multi – axis Dynamic Endurance Test Machine with Moog Control Systems
  • Computerized Universal Testing Machines
  • Computerized Low Temperature, Environmental Conditioning Test Chambers and Ozone Test Equipment
  • Rheometers for testing Rubber Mix Compounds
  • Fire Retardant Test Equipment as per UIC / ASTM standards
  • Static Load Deflection and Bend Test Equipment for Polymers and Steel
  • Computerized Functional and Leakage Test Benches for LPG hoses

Our policy of following the most stringent Quality Assurance Plans (QAP) for all our products ensures quality, consistency and traceability.

About Company

An ISO-9001-2015 Quality System certified company, Basant Rubber Factory develops, manufactures and markets wide range of rubber extruded, moulded and rubber to metal bonded products. These products, known for their quality and performance, are extensively used in Indian Households, Railways, Automobile, Defence and Civil Engineering industries.
