Our Clients

Basant Rubber Factory Private Limited has worked with leading Indian and international companies spread across various sectors such as oil and natural gas, defence, Railways, shipping and marine and civil. Some of the names include:

  • Indian Railways
  • Indian Oil Corporation Limited
  • Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited
  • Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited
  • Diesel Locomotive Works (DLW)
  • Chittaranjan Locomotive Works

Basant Rubber Factory Private Limited has maintained strong and on-going relations with many of its clients. Their continuous association with the company is a reflection of the quality extended by Basant Rubber Factory.

Going forward, the company promises its clients that it will continue to stand committed to its quality assurance and provide the highest grade of rubber products at all times.

About Company

An ISO-9001-2015 Quality System certified company, Basant Rubber Factory develops, manufactures and markets wide range of rubber extruded, moulded and rubber to metal bonded products. These products, known for their quality and performance, are extensively used in Indian Households, Railways, Automobile, Defence and Civil Engineering industries.
