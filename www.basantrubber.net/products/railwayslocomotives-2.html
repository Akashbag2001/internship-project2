Locomotives

Basant Rubber Factory Private Ltd. develops and manufactures rubber pads, rubber supports, spherical bearings and side bearer springs used in all classes of electric and diesel locomotives in the Indian Railways.

Our side bearer spring pads and sandwich mountings are used for secondary suspension application in locomotives such as WAG-9, WAG-7, WAG-5, WAP-7, WAP- 5, WAP-4, WDM-2, WDM-3D, WDG-4 and WDP-4. These pads are subjected to static and dynamic loadings both in compressive and shear modes simultaneously in operating condition.

We also manufacture spherical bearings and silent blocks for anchor link, vibration control and coupling applications in locomotives and passenger coaches and thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) spring pads for sealing and vibration controls. The design, dimensions and load characteristics of our products can be changed to suit the customer’s requirements.

We supply to major locomotive manufacturers in India such as Chittaranjan Locomotive Works (CLW), Diesel Modernization Works (DMW), Patiala and Diesel Locomotive Works (DLW), Varanasi.

About Company

An ISO-9001-2015 Quality System certified company, Basant Rubber Factory develops, manufactures and markets wide range of rubber extruded, moulded and rubber to metal bonded products. These products, known for their quality and performance, are extensively used in Indian Households, Railways, Automobile, Defence and Civil Engineering industries.
